A Statistical Analysis
of His Climbing Career

This site shares information about the number of first ascents & new routes that Fred Beckey climbed, along with who he climbed with. It also includes a list of routes & peaks named after him, in addition to data on where he climbed. The site contains lists of climbs he made during his career too.

The various statistical analysis articles are labeled as Chapters. You don't need to read them in order. But I recommend you read Chapter 1, first. Before reading Chapter 11 about Beckey's Greatest Climbing Year, I recommend you review Chapters 8-10, to familiarize yourself with lists of climbs in North America and Washington State, indicating the quality or difficulty of routes or peaks.  These three lists are  key critieria used to help determine Beckey's greatest climbing year. Chapters 13-15 contain summit register entries which should be of historical interest and prove to be entertaining. Also check out the quiz game about Fred Beckey.

Enjoy and as I state in Chapter 1, I look forward to receiving input from Beckey's climbing partners to improve the completeness and accuracy of his list of climbs and the statistics.
By: Dave Creeden

However, you will need to provide me evidence. This can be in the form of a climbing journal or dairy entry. Also, a trip report, photographs, video, summit register entry, are acceptable pieces of evidence.

The key information that is lacking for some of Beckey’s climbs are:
• Specific date
• Climbing route
• Other people in the climbing party
